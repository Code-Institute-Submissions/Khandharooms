The Studio

Control Room

The control room here at Khandha is approximately 5.5m (18ft) x 3m (10ft). At the center lies a high performance mac and is fitted with all major DAWS and a colossal folder of plugins, software synths and samples. The outboard rack houses an Aurora 16 Converter, providing ultra high clarity and performance up to 192k. Preamps from Audient, Pheonix Audio, Sytek and SPL offer a selection of colours whilst a small but quality collection of compressors from AMEK, Universal Audio and DBX add some weight.
The control room also has the space for relaxation whilst listening back to recordings on a pair of JBL LSR 4328 foldback monitors , a bathroom and viewport window into the live room. The general comfortable vibe along with crystal clear production will keep you coming back for more.

Live Room

The live room is also approximately 5.5m (18ft) x 3m (10ft) being the perfect size for solo artists, duos and up to a five musicians to record at one time, there is also plenty of space for groups of voice actors or comedians to lay tracks. The walls and ceiling are dampened by bass traps, creating a perfectly dead room and removing any unwanted frequency. The room itself has a warm and homely ambience, encompassing comfort to create a relaxed environment for clients to be able to perform without feeling under pressure.
