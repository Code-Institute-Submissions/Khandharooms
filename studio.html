The Studio

Control Room

The control room here at Khandha is approximatley 5.5m (18ft) x 3m (10ft). At the center lies a high performance mac and is fitted with all major DAWS and a colossal folder of plugins, software synths and samples. The outboard rack houses an Aurora 16 Converterproviding ultra high clarity and performance up to 192k. Preamps from Audient, Pheonix Audio , Sytek and SPL offer a selection of colours whilst a small but quailty collection of compressors from AMEK, Universal Audio and DBX add some weight.
The control room also has the space for relaxtion whilst listening back to recordings on a pair of JBL LSR 4328 foldback monitors , a bathroom and viewport window into the live room. Its general comfortable vibe along with crystal clear production will keep you coming back for more.
