About Us

Zac Dorne (Studio Owner)

The Story

Tucked away in a crooked old stable hidden behind a row of shops on leigh broadway Khandha Rooms houses a powerful collection of high end recording equipment, instruments and amps for all musicians needs! The building has been used to make records of all shapes and sizes for more than 20 years. Rooting heavily from the Southend reggae scene the studio has grown into a home for musicians from all walks of life and has an extensive repertoire that spans across a cavern of genres.
