Services

Mixing

Getting a great mix is important for any track be it a demo or release, no one wants to hear a muddy unbalanced mess or someone shouting "run that again" in the middle of a song!
With a deep knowledge of how effects work, correct use of EQ, compression and balancing of tracks, this ensures you get the perfect blend to make your track pop!
These are just a few of the many reasons why people choose Khandha.

Record Production

Zac Dorne, owner and engineer, has the skills and ear capable of producing your Single, EP or Album to the highest quality. For those seeking record production, Zac will oversee and manage the sound comfortably and confidently, whilst suggesting new ideas to get the best out of the artist. Time management, mixing and mastering will be included in the service giving assurance and allowing you to fully embrace your music.

Mastering

The term mastering may be new to you and you may be wondering what its all about or why people charge a pretty penny to "make your song louder".
Mastering is a dark art that brings a track to life, perfecting it for the world to hear. The difference between mastered and unmasterd music is drastic and highly noticeable, here at khandha we take our time to fully understand what you want and need from the final master. Whether you want your project to be "LOUD" to match other artists in your genere, or whether you want to enchance dynamic variations to complement the progression, Khandha can help you achieve any sound you are looking for.
A free test master is offered to all new clients. Any tweaks or changes are included until you are 100% happy.
